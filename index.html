Our Story

Nestled in the lush greenery of Kerala, Kottiyoor Valley Park & Resort was founded with a simple mission: to provide a peaceful sanctuary for families and nature lovers. Far from the city noise, we offer an authentic atmosphere where the air is fresh and the vibes are serene.


Our Philosophy: We believe in "Nature First." Our resort is designed to blend seamlessly with the environment, offering family-friendly hospitality that feels like home.
